How to Get Red River Hostel ?

Map & directions
To come to RED RIVER Hostel from Ataturk Airport, you may find your way to Taksim Tophane or Taksim Tunel

1) Take the Subway from Ataturk Airport to Aksaray (Last Stop) .
When you come out of subway cross the main street towards to the Tramline and take the Tram from Yusufpasa Tram Stop to Tophane Station.

From Tophane station take the street calls Kumbaraci yokusu and climb the street like 100 meters after you will see Red River Hostel on your left hand side we are No:35

2.) You can get our from tram at Karakoy Station and you can take historical metro (calls Tunel) to get to the end point of Istiklal street.

After get in to Istiklal street if you will walk up about 150 meters on your right hand side you will see Kumbaraci Yokusu Street. Get in to Kumbaraci yokusu and walk 100 meters down on your right hand side you will find Red River Hostel. Our building is No:35

3.) Take a Public Bus nearby the Yusufpasa Tram Stop to Taksim Square
And than;

Walk into the Istiklal Street and just before end of the istiklal street after the Russian consulate turn left to (Kumbaraci Yokusu street) 100 m way down you will see Red River Hostel on your right hand side.Our building is No:35

4.) Take HAVAS bus from any airport to Taksim square
(There are 2 airports in Istanbul Sabiha Gokcen airport on the Asian side and Ataturk airport on the European side)
Than again,

Walk in to the Istiklal Street and just before end of the Istiklal Street after the Russian consulate turn left to (Kumbaraci Yokusu street) 100 m way down you will see Red River Hostel on your right side.Our building is No:35


You ask for a Airport Pick-up and we arrange it for you.
